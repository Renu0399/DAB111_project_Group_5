About Lung Cancer Data

The data is sourced from "https://www.kaggle.com/code/rashadrmammadov/exploratory-analysis-of-synthetic-lung-cancer-data/input"

Definitions of each variable:

  • Patient_ID: Unique identifier for each patient (data type: integer)
  • Age: Age of the patient (data type: integer)
  • Gender: Gender of the patient (data type: string)
  • Smoking_History: Smoking history of the patient (data type: string)
  • Tumor_Size_mm: Size of the tumor in millimeters (data type: float)
  • Tumor_Location: Location of the tumor (data type: string)
  • Stage: Stage of cancer (data type: string)
  • Treatment: Treatment method (data type: string)
  • Survival_Months: Survival months (data type: integer)
  • Ethnicity: Ethnicity of the patient (data type: string)
  • Insurance_Type: Type of insurance (data type: string)
  • Family_History: Family history of cancer (data type: string)
